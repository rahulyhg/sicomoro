PRADEEP DOKANIA - Partner

Mr. Pradeep Dokania has over 29 years of experience in the Indian financial industry. An alumnus of the Indian Institute of Management - Ahmedabad, Mr. Dokania joined DSP Merrill Lynch Limited in 1986 as an investment banker, and on to head the Investment Banking, Fixed Income and Private Clients business functions.

He has been the Chairman of India Global Wealth Management at Merrill Lynch and was part of the team that set up the India Wealth Management business at Merrill Lynch.

As an Executive Committee and Board member, he has been an integral part of the evolution and exciting growth of the financial markets in India.

AMIT KHANDELWAL - Partner

Mr. Amit Khandelwal is a Chartered Accountant with 23 years of experience in the banking industry. Through the course of his career, he has been associated with prestigious brands such as DSP Merrill Lynch, Credit Suisse and Kotak Bank, serving as Relationship Manager, Team Leader, National Sales Head, and finally emerging as one of the most respected private bankers in the industry.

He has not only managed prolific client portfolios in India, but has also successfully handled NRI accounts across the Middle East, Africa and Europe/UK. Amit brings to the table his rich experience in Market Research and Risk Management, and astute understanding of Fixed Income & Equity Operations, New Business Initiatives and Wealth Management.

RAKESH BHATIA - Partner

Mr. Rakesh I Bhatia is a Chartered Accountant and a Company Secretary with 24 years of experience in Financial Services, IT and Consumer facing industries. He has worked with various Reputed Indian Corporates and Multinational Companies in India and abroad. Before co-founding Sicomoro Advisors, he worked with Tata Capital as Business Head of the Rural Business. He has also held pivotal roles with American Express, IDBI, i-flex Solutions (Oracle), Tupperware and Yamaha.

He brings with him a rich experience in Financial Services, Risk Management, IT Solutions and Process Excellence.

NILESH GANJWALA - Partner

Mr. Nilesh Ganjwala, a Chartered Accountant and veteran with over 30 years of industry experience spearheads Innergize, a renowned strategy and management consultancy.
